Zesty Southwest Corn Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen corn, thawed and drained
  • 1 1/2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/3 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablesspoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh line juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine corn, tomatoes, bell pepper, and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk olive oil, cilantro, vinegar, lime juice, chilli powder, cumin, garlic powder, and cayenne together in a small bowl. Add dressing to corn mixture just before serving and toss well.
